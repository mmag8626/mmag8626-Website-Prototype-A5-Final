Lives Together

Pets of the Homeless Australia provides supplies, veterinary care, temporary homes and other necessities for homeless peoples pets or the financially vulnerable.

The number of surrendered or abandoned pets sent to be euthanised at shelters can be reduced with the protection of keeping companions together.

Our Approach

Food

Together, as of May 2020, we have accommodated 190,225 meals to the animals owned by the homeless to ensure their food security.

Health

Peta reported 6 million animals each year are in animal shelters as caretakers lack financial support for veterinary care of, cancerous tumours, contagious diseases, and others.

Shelter

Homeless owners sometimes have to make sacrifices to leave their pet on the streets if they want a chance to even sleep one night in a hostel or rental home.
